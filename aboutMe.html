Background

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for front-end development. I grew up with a mind for engineering. When I would receive a new console or gaming system, I was always more interested in how it worked. I would always take it apart to figure out how it worked. More often than not, I would typically break the system, but my curiosity never went away. As I grew, I learned more about how to put it all back together. I even managed to find out where all the extra parts were supposed to go. My engineering mindset progressed as I performed regular maintenance on my consoles, computers, and vehicles.

I started my pursuit for development with the University of North Carolina's Full Stack Boot Camp. During this program, I built various applications using Javascript and MERN Stack. One of my favorite projects to work on was making Liri. It was designed to act as a personal helper. It takes written commands to find concerts for your favorite artist or specific music from spotify. It uses API integration to pull in the information and displays in on the command line. This was a great segway into AI and computer learning.
