About me

I am an emerging full stack engineer with a background in healthcare. I have always had a passion for technology since I was a child. I grew up with a mind for engineering. When I would receive a new console or gaming system I would always try to take it apart to figure out how it worked. More often than not, I would typically break the system but my curiosity never went away. As I grew, I learned more about how to put it all back together. Even managed to find out where all the extra parts were supposed to go. I performed regular maintenance on my consoles, computers and even vehicles just to learn more about how everything worked.

I started my pursuit for development with University of North Carolina's Full Stack Boot Camp. During this program, I built various applications using the various different technologies listed below. One of my favorite projects to work on was building Liri. It is a fun project where you have endless possibilities on features to add. It really showcases the power of Node.js and Artificial intelligence.
